Donald Trump Coronavirus Press Conference Transcript April 16: Guidelines to Reopen Parts of US in Phases

President Donald Trump’s April 16 coronavirus press conference. Trump outlined guidelines to reopen parts of the U.S. in phases: Phase 1, Phase 2, and Phase 3. Phase 1 requires most of the current lockdown measures (avoiding non-essential travel, not gathering in groups).
