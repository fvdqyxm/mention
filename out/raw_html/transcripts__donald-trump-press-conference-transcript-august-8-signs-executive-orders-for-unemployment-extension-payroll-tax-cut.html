Donald Trump Transcript 8/8: Signs Executive Orders for Unemployment Extension & Payroll Tax Cut

Donald Trump held an August 8 press conference in which he signed executive orders at Trump National Golf Club Bedminster. The orders extend unemployment & provide $400 benefits for Americans out of work due to the coronavirus pandemic. One order also would provide a payroll tax cut for people earning less than $100,000 per year. Trump walked out, leaving the press conference early after a reporter’s question.
