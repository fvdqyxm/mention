A vote for President Trump means the future of the automobile will be made in America where it should be. Made in America.

Hopefully, your leaders at United Auto Workers will endorse Donald Trump because standard history for years and decades and years and years and years, they always endorse a Democrat.

(14:38)

Now we have the worst border in the history of the world, by the way.

(15:02)

Nearly a quarter of a million jobs were destroyed here in Michigan alone, including 40% of all auto jobs. Think of that. In fact, I had to check this number because it’s inconceivable, you would think. 60,000 factories closed and went overseas. Can you imagine 60,000? Now, who would think that?

(15:35)
When I first saw that number, I said, “Oh, somebody made a typographical error. Maybe it was 600 factories.” Could have been 6,000, but that sounded too high. 60,000. The good news is if the number is wrong, the fake news will be checking it out. I mean I have to give you the right numbers. Otherwise, the fake news is out there.

(15:56)

But it was the men and women who got … Every single day, they got up and came back home with grease in their hands, and they were the ones that paid the price. They paid a big, big price.

(16:19)

Based on what we’re seeing, it’s much more often than anyone would have thought. But can you imagine an unannounced raid on his many homes? He would have made Senator Menendez look like a baby, just like a baby. Can you imagine?

(16:41)
They gave him three weeks’ notice. They said, “We’re going to raid your home over documents.” They gave him three weeks. Would three weeks be enough? I can imagine. I’d like to see what he cleaned out of there. Joe Biden only cares about enriching his own family. I care about enriching your family. That’s why I did this. That’s why I did it. I’m working for you, not for me. That is for sure. I always will. I will always have your back. I promise you that. I will always have your back.

(17:12)
When I came into office, the auto industry was on its knees, gasping its last breaths after eight long years of Obama and Biden. But you finally got a president who stood up to the … You got to understand, I stood up to people that hate you. They hate you or they maybe hate our country. But I stood up for you. I stood up for the auto workers and stood up for the great state of Michigan like nobody’s ever stood up before.

(17:46)
Just as I promised, I withdrew from the Trans-Pacific Partnership my very first week. Do you remember 2016 when we won Michigan? Michigan hadn’t been won in decades, and everybody said, “Aw.”

Donald Trump (18:00):

And then, we did much better in 2020. I mean, I hate to tell you, we did much better. But we’re going to swamp them. We’re going to swamp them. We actually did much better, including in Michigan. I ended Obama’s war on American energy, he had a war on American energy, gasoline prices plummeted and soon reached $1 and 87 cents a gallon. How does that sound? Back in 2008, Biden and Obama promised you that they’d renegotiate NAFTA, but in eight years they never lifted a finger. They never changed a thing.

(19:49)

(20:21)

(20:40)

Right now, it’s probably the strongest part of your industry. Without that, you wouldn’t have an industry. So, it’s something I was very proud to do. It was not easy, but we got it done and we got it done properly.

(21:23)
Michigan jobs would’ve been exported while millions and millions of foreign cars would’ve flooded American streets. That’s what would’ve happened. Obama and Biden didn’t care one bit, but I did and I stopped it and I stopped it cold. I took on communist China like no administration in history. No administration in history did what I’ve done, bringing in hundreds of billions of dollars pouring into our treasury when no other president had gotten literally 10 cents.

Not because they wanted to, but because they have no choice.

(23:24)

But crooked Joe Biden is back like a wretched old vulture trying to finish off his prey. Can you believe it?
